# Product Requirements

## Goal

Collect and assess social content relating to Gildan brands, beginning with American Apparel. Distinguish current-product content from legacy/throwback content, preserve decision evidence, and provide a shared human-review workflow.

## Users

Gildan social and community team members.

## Core jobs

1. Collect candidate posts and comments.
2. Analyze caption, metadata, visuals, audio and on-screen text.
3. Route uncertain items to human review.
4. Allow authenticated colleagues to share one database.
5. Preserve automated and human decisions.
6. Run auditable date-range scrapes.
7. Export reviewed data for analysis and model improvement.

## Content identity

A post and each comment beneath it are separate content items. Comments must retain a parent reference.

## Review interface

Each item should show:

- source link;
- platform and author;
- content type;
- parent relationship;
- caption;
- every carousel image;
- representative video frames;
- transcript;
- OCR;
- automated classification and confidence;
- evidence by channel;
- reason it entered review;
- human decision and notes;
- audit history.

## Shared state

Production must include:

- authentication;
- roles;
- central database;
- concurrent-review protection;
- reviewer identity;
- timestamps;
- audit trail.
